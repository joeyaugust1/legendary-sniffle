How to Play

Objective: Balance the economy by achieving 2+ win conditions while avoiding loss conditions!

Win Conditions: Achieve economic stability (inflation 1-3%, unemployment <5%), create a budget surplus, reduce debt below 60%, or hit the "Goldilocks Economy" (all indicators optimal).

Loss Conditions: Avoid severe recession (unemployment >8%), hyperinflation (>15%), debt crisis (>130%), stock market crash (>50% drop), or civil unrest (confidence <20).
